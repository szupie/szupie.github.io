I’m Angus Lo, an interaction designer

I use design and technology to make things easier to understand for people. I have been coding since the age of 7, when I built my first website on Geocities. Most of my work is digital – including websites, AR/VR, and other tools and experiences. I have also designed physical products and installations informed by my knowledge of ergonomics and engineering constraints.

I am obsessed with understanding how things work. My expertise covers a wide variety of fields from design to sciences, making me uniquely adept at finding obscure connections, potential problems, and novel solutions that others miss.

I am currently based in New York City, working as an Associate Creative Director of experience design at Havas. My clients include IBM, American Airlines, NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital, Keurig, Air Wick, and more.
