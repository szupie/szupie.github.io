Investment account dashboard

Reimagining an investment dashboard to improve discoverability of product suite

Roles

  • Interaction Design

This work is from my role as an experience designer at an advertising agency

In this project, I reimagined the app homescreen for an investment management service, looking for ways to promote the suite of tools offered. The concept design encourages users to take control of their investments via contextual and personalised action recommendations.

Ecosystem mapping

I started with research on the company’s ecosystem of investment tools and services. Like many companies with an established history, the product organisation was influenced by company structure, and reorganisation based on relevant user needs would improve discoverability.

Dashboard concept

Based on the research, I designed an app homescreen concept organised by user intents, starting from the most common and basic and advancing to more involved actions:

  • Monitor their portfolio
  • Update their portfolio with recommended actions
  • Invest in new funds
  • Research larger market trends
